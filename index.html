Welcome to Farming School..!

Welcome to Farm to School, a transformative program dedicated to bringing fresh, locally-produced foods into school cafeterias while educating students about nutrition, sustainability, and the importance of supporting local agriculture. Our initiative bridges the gap between farms and schools, providing students with access to healthy meals, hands-on learning experiences, and a deeper understanding of where their food comes from. By fostering these connections, we aim to improve student health, bolster local economies, and create a more sustainable food system for future generations. Join us in our mission to cultivate healthy kids, thriving farms, and vibrant communities.
